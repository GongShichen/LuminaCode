Design for dense engineering work:

- Clear hierarchy, readable labels, restrained color.
- Stable layout across narrow and wide terminals.
- Animated active states without distracting motion.
- Dialogue blocks with enough spacing and attribution.
- Dark-mode friendly contrast.
