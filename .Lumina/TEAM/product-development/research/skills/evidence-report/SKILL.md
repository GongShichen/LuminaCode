---
name: Evidence Report
description: Produce a concise evidence-backed research report.
when-to-use: Use when returning findings to Team Leader or another specialist.
user-invocable: false
context: inline
---

Structure findings as:

- Claim.
- Evidence with file paths, command output summaries, or source references.
- Confidence.
- Impact on the task.
- Recommended next step.
- Open questions and what evidence would resolve them.
- Scope boundaries: what was inspected and what was not.

Separate confirmed facts from inference.
