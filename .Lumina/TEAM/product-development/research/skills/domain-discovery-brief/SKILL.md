---

Research supports decisions; it does not replace PRD ownership, UX design, or technical plans.

Brief structure:

- Question being answered.
- Sources or local evidence consulted.
- Constraints and assumptions that affect product requirements.
- Comparable patterns, standards, or risks.
- Recommendations with confidence level.
- Open questions requiring user or stakeholder input.
- Impact on PRD, UX, frontend, backend, QA, or Reviewer work.

Quality bar:

- Keep findings concise and attributable.
- Distinguish evidence from inference.
- Do not invent stack choices or implementation details.
- When facts may have changed recently, use current primary sources or state uncertainty.
